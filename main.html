What are you studying? What is "Mathematics of Computation"?

Mathematics of Computation (abbreviated as "Math of Comp") is a BS in the Letters and Science College under the math department. We study a lot of math, but we also many CS courses. These CS courses include data structures, algorithms, introduction to computer organization, and software construction. We also have the option to take many statistics courses, including probabilty and courses related to the software language R. Basically, it's the best of math, CS, and statistics. It's a great pathway to software engineering, data science, machine learning, and countless other fields!
